# Developer Resources

Welcome to the Developer Resources section! As you move beyond the basics, you'll need a deeper understanding of the tools and concepts that power Web3 development. This section is designed to be a growing repository of guides and references for the modern Web3 developer.

## What You'll Find Here

-   **Smart Contracts**: An introduction to Solidity, the most popular language for writing smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain.
-   **Development Frameworks**: Overviews of powerful frameworks like Hardhat and Foundry that streamline the development, testing, and deployment of smart contracts.
-   **Connecting to the Blockchain**: A closer look at RPC providers and the libraries you'll use to communicate with the blockchain from your frontend.

This section will be continuously updated with more content. If there's a topic you'd like to see covered, feel free to open an issue or submit a pull request!
